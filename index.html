Your Installation. Our Responsibility.

You have enough on your plate. Here's how we take the furniture installation off of it.

01

Consult & Plan

We start by listening. Our project managers meet with you to understand the scope, walk the site, and build a clear plan — so you know exactly what to expect and when.

02

Deliver & Handle with Care

Your furniture arrives on our trucks, handled by our crew. Every piece is inventoried, inspected, and protected. Nothing gets to your site without us knowing about it first.

Install & Walk Away Ready

Our technicians install everything to manufacturer specs and test every component. We walk the finished space with you, address any punch list items, and don't leave until you're satisfied.
