Favicon Direction

Which approach best represents HD Installations? Favicons are 32x32px — simplicity is key.

A — Monogram "HD"

Classic serif monogram in gold on charcoal. Clean, professional, reads well at small sizes. Matches the DM Serif Display branding on the site.

B — Bold "H" on Gold

Single letter on gold background. Maximum simplicity — extremely legible at favicon size. Inverts the site's color hierarchy for tab distinction.

C — Abstract Furniture Icon

Minimal desk/table silhouette in gold on charcoal. Communicates the industry at a glance. Risk: may be too abstract at 16x16px.

D — Shield Monogram

HD letters inside a shield outline. References the trust/licensed/bonded messaging on the site. Adds visual weight but may be busy at small sizes.
